This guide will walk you through creating an Azure account using a Credit Card.
Goal:Get $200 USD (or equivalent) credit + 12 months of free services.

Prerequisites:A valid Microsoft Account (Outlook/Hotmail), a Mobile Phone, and a standard Credit Card (Visa/MasterCard/Amex).

Step 1:Go to the Official Sign-Up Page
       Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox)
       Go to azure.microsoft.com/free
       Click on the green "Start free" button.
       Warning: Do not click "Pay as you go" or "Buy now." You want the specific "Free Account" offer.

Step 2:Sign In / Create Identity
       Sign In:You will be prompted to log in.Use your existing Microsoft email (e.g., user@outlook.com).
       If you don't have one: Click "Create one!" to set up a new email address instantly.
       Profile Info: Fill in the "About you" section:
       First & Last Name: Must match your ID/Credit Card exactly.
       Email: Your main email.
       Phone: Your mobile number.

Step 3:Identity Verification (Phone)
       Select your country code (e.g., +91 or +1).
       Enter your mobile number.
       Click "Text me".
       Enter the 6-digit verification code you receive via SMS.

Step 4:Identity Verification (Credit Card)
       This is the most critical step where most users face issues.
       Select "Credit/Debit Card" as the payment method.
       Enter Card Details:Card Number, Expiry Date, CVV.
       Cardholder Name:Enter it exactly as it appears on the physical card.
       Address:Ensure the billing address matches the address your bank has on file.

Step 5:Final Agreement
       Check the box that says "I agree to the Microsoft Customer Agreement."
       (Optional) Uncheck the box for marketing emails if you don't want them.
       Click the "Sign Up" button.
